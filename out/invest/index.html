Project Bathan - Sustainable Beef Production

Building a healthier, more ethical livestock ecosystem.

Project Bathan is Afeelaa Farms' flagship initiative in sustainable, halal, and ethical cattle farming. We source local (deshi) cows and oxen directly from trusted farmers, supporting rural communities while ensuring full traceability. Each animal is carefully examined by our veterinary experts to confirm its health and suitability before being brought to our farm.

At Bathan, we follow natural feeding practices, provide clean water and open grazing, and maintain a stress-free environment. This ethical process results in premium-quality halal meat, improved animal welfare, and sustainable environmental management.

Beyond production, Bathan contributes to employment generation, skills development, and community well-being - proving that responsible farming can deliver both profit and positive impact.

Project Fawakih - Sustainable Fruits & Crops

Cultivating nature's sweetness with purpose.

Project Fawakih is one of Afeelaa Farms' most promising ventures - a long-term initiative dedicated to growing premium, naturally nurtured fruits across the fertile lands of Chapai Nawabganj. This region's rich soil and ideal climate make it perfect for cultivating Mango, Jackfruit, Dragon Fruit, and Guava through sustainable and eco-balanced methods.

Our farming approach blends modern agro-technology with traditional cultivation wisdom, using balanced nutrition, natural composting, and Global Gap Certified minimal chemical support only when necessary for crop protection. This ensures healthy, high-quality produce while maintaining soil vitality and ecological balance.

Beyond farming, Project Fawakih creates local employment, provides training in sustainable agriculture, and strengthens Bangladesh's reputation for quality fruit exports - all while offering investors an ethical and transparent way to grow their capital.

Project Matsya - Sustainable Fish Farming

Nurturing life below water, responsibly.

Project Matsya represents Afeelaa Farms' vision of eco-conscious and halal-driven aquaculture, where profitability aligns with sustainability. Located in carefully managed freshwater environments, this project focuses on responsible fish farming through a balance of modern aquaculture systems and natural processes.

Our ponds function as self-sustaining ecosystems, where water quality, oxygen levels, and aquatic biodiversity are closely maintained. Fish are raised using nutrient-rich, plant-based feed and scientific care, while maintaining GLOBALG.A.P.-standard levels of chemical use - ensuring health, safety, and environmental responsibility.

Through this model, Project Matsya produces high-quality, nutritious fish, preserves aquatic balance, and delivers consistent halal-based returns to investors. Beyond production, it also creates employment opportunities, provides training for rural families, and strengthens Bangladesh's food security and export potential.
